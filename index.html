RAM KRISHNA VIDYA MANDIR HIGH SCHOOL,MAKANDPUR,BHAGALPUR is a cbse and co_educational institute having classes from nursury to 12 with about 3300 students on its roll.It has a pride to nestle in a serene and holy town enticing multitude. of people from all over the countery and even abroad with its rich past associated with SWAMI VIVEKANAND JI. The school is located at Rannuchack on Bhaglpur away from hustle and bustle,amid the lush green surrounding.It is about 1.5 km from the general bus stand of bhavnathpur.The school has its own giant campus with spacious double storey blocks of primary and senior wings.
